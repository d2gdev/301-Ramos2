Our Story

2020
Idea/Opening

With a worldwide pandemic right around the corner, Nelly and Mark decided to take ownership of 301 Ramos Bar and Grill. The first 18 months were a blast, but also stressful. Thankfully, we made it through the days of ECQ, MECQ, MGCQ, etc. We made it through because of our brave and loyal customers.

2021
Expansion

After getting started and spending lots of great days and nights upstairs, we decided we needed some extra space, and took over the space next door. This allowed us to move the bands downstairs, and give us some much needed street view. The knockon effect of this has been to help make us a hub for local musicans, and a more friendly place to spend time.

2022
Today

Join us for unbeatable daily drink specials, quality food, and every sports package imaginable. Where is the best place in Cebu to watch live sports? 301 Ramos Bar and Grill.
